## Common Questions

### Do I need to download an app?

No! AceUnlock works in your web browser. Just open the link in the invitation email on any device with a camera and microphone.

### What devices can I use?

- ğŸ“± Smartphone (iOS or Android)
- ğŸ’» Laptop (Windows or Mac)
- ğŸ–¥ï¸ Desktop computer
- ğŸ“² Tablet (iPad or Android)

### When can I interview?

You can interview anytime within the deadline specified in your invitation email (usually 3-7 days). The interview is available 24/7.

### How long does the interview take?

Most interviews take **15-30 minutes**. The exact length depends on the role and your responses (the AI asks follow-up questions).

### Can I redo my interview?

Generally, no. You get one opportunity to complete the interview. This ensures authenticity and fairness. Make sure you're in a quiet place with good lighting before you start.

### What if I have technical issues?

Contact support immediately: support@aceunlock.com. We can help with camera/microphone issues, browser compatibility, and other technical problems.

### Is this a live interview?

No. The AI Interviewer asks questions via audio, and you respond on video. However, the AI is **adaptive** - it listens to your responses and asks relevant follow-up questions. You can also ask the AI questions about the role.

### What happens after I complete the interview?

1. Your interview is automatically saved
2. You'll see a confirmation/thank you page
3. You'll receive a confirmation email
4. The hiring team reviews your interview
5. They'll contact you if they want to proceed
