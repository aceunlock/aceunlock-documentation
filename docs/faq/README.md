# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AceUnlock.

## Getting Started

**How long does setup take?**
Most users complete initial setup in under 30 minutes. Enterprise customers with SSO may need 1-2 hours.

**Do I need technical knowledge to use AceUnlock?**
No. AceUnlock is designed for non-technical users. However, advanced features like API integration may require developer assistance.

**Can I try before buying?**
Yes! We offer a free plan with core features. No credit card required.

[Create Free Account](../getting-started/create-account.md)

---

## Account & Billing

**How do I upgrade my plan?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Changes take effect immediately.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit card, ACH transfer (Enterprise only), and annual invoicing (Enterprise only).

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing settings. No cancellation fees.

**Do you offer refunds?**
We offer refunds within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied.

## Security

**Is my data encrypted?**
Yes. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

**Where are your servers located?**
Primary servers in US-East (Virginia) and EU-West (Ireland). You can choose your region.

**Are you SOC 2 compliant?**
Yes. Our SOC 2 Type II report is available to Enterprise customers.

**Do you support SSO?**
Yes. SAML-based SSO is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

[Learn More: Security](../features/security-settings.md)

## Features

**What's the difference between roles and permissions?**
Roles are pre-defined sets of permissions (Admin, Member, Guest). Permissions are granular access controls.

**Can I create custom roles?**
Yes, on Enterprise plans. Pro plans have 5 pre-defined roles.

**How many team members can I add?**
- Free: 3 members
- Pro: 25 members
- Enterprise: Unlimited

**Do you have a mobile app?**
Not yet, but our web interface is mobile-responsive. Native apps coming in 2025.

## Troubleshooting

**I forgot my password**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset via email.

[Password Reset Guide](../troubleshooting/password-reset.md)

**I'm not receiving emails**
Check your spam folder. Add noreply@aceunlock.com to your contacts.

**My 2FA isn't working**
Use a backup code or contact support for account recovery.

**Can I import users from another system?**
Yes. We support CSV import and API-based migration.

**Will I lose data when switching plans?**
No. All your data is preserved when upgrading or downgrading.

**How long does migration take?**
Depends on data volume. Most migrations complete in under 24 hours.
