# User Profile

The User Profile page allows you to view and manage your personal account information and subscription details on AceUnlock.

## Overview

The User Profile page is organized into two main tabs:

- **My Profile**: View and manage your personal information and account details
- **Subscription**: View your subscription plan and billing information

## My Profile Tab

### Profile Header

The top section of your profile displays:

- **Avatar**: Your profile picture or default avatar icon
- **Email Address**: Your registered email
- **User ID**: Your unique user identifier in the system

### Basic Information

The Basic Information section displays your account details:

- **Full Name**: Your first and last name
- **Email**: Your registered email address
- **Email Verified**: Verification status
  - **Yes**: Your email has been verified
  - **No**: Your email requires verification

### Email Verification

If your email shows as not verified:

1. Check your inbox for a verification email from AceUnlock
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. If you didn't receive the email:
   - Check your spam/junk folder
   - Request a new verification email from your profile page
   - Contact support@aceunlock.com if issues persist

**Why verification matters**:
- Ensures you receive important notifications
- Required for password reset functionality
- Verifies account ownership for security

## Subscription Tab

### Viewing Subscription Details

The Subscription tab displays comprehensive information about your current plan:

#### Plan Information
- **Plan Name**: Your active subscription tier (e.g., Growth, Business, or Enterprise)
- **Subscription ID**: Unique identifier for your subscription
- **Start Date**: When your subscription began
- **End Date**: When your subscription expires (blank if ongoing/no end date)
- **Plan Amount**: Your monthly or annual subscription cost

#### Available Credits

The subscription tab shows your available credits for key features:

1. **Screening Interview**: 
   - Total interviews available in your plan
   - Remaining interview credits for the current billing period

2. **Job Postings**: 
   - Total job roles you can have active
   - Remaining job posting slots available

3. **Team Members**: 
   - Total team member seats included in your plan
   - Available seats for inviting new members

### Understanding Credits

- **Credits Reset Monthly**: Most credits refresh at the start of each billing cycle
- **Overage Charges**: Using more than your allotted credits may incur additional fees ($5 per interview over quota)
- **Real-Time Updates**: Credit balances update immediately as you use features

### Managing Your Subscription

For detailed subscription management including:
- Changing your plan
- Viewing detailed usage statistics
- Managing billing information
- Viewing invoice history

Visit the [Company Management](./company.md) page (Admin access required).

**Note**: Only Admin users can modify subscription plans and access full billing details. If you're a Recruiter and need plan changes, contact your organization's Admin.

## Updating Your Profile

### Changing Your Name

To update your name:

1. Navigate to the **My Profile** tab
2. Click the **Edit** icon next to Basic Information
3. Update your first and/or last name
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Changing Your Email

**Important**: Email changes may require reverification.

To update your email address:

1. Navigate to the **My Profile** tab
2. Click the **Edit** icon next to Basic Information
3. Enter your new email address
4. Click **"Save Changes"**
5. Check your new email for a verification link
6. Click the verification link to confirm the change

### Updating Your Avatar

To change your profile picture:

1. Navigate to the **My Profile** tab
2. Hover over your current avatar
3. Click the **Upload** or **Change** icon
4. Select an image from your computer
5. Crop or adjust the image if needed
6. Click **"Save"** to update

**Avatar Requirements**:
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Square images work best

## Account Security

### Password Management

To change your password:

1. Navigate to the **My Profile** tab
2. Look for the **Security** or **Password** section
3. Click **"Change Password"**
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password
6. Confirm your new password
7. Click **"Update Password"**

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Two-Factor Authentication

For enhanced security, consider enabling two-factor authentication (if available):

1. Go to Security settings in your profile
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Follow the setup instructions
4. Save your backup codes in a secure location

## Notification Preferences

### Managing Email Notifications

Control which emails you receive from AceUnlock:

1. Navigate to **My Profile** tab
2. Find the **Notifications** or **Email Preferences** section
3. Toggle notifications on/off for:
   - Interview completions
   - New candidate applications
   - Team member invitations
   - System updates and announcements
   - Billing and subscription updates
4. Click **"Save Preferences"**

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Update Profile Information

**Issue**: Edit button is grayed out or changes won't save

**Solution**:
- Refresh the page and try again
- Clear your browser cache
- Ensure all required fields are filled in correctly
- Check that you're connected to the internet
- Try a different browser
- Contact support@aceunlock.com if issue persists

### Email Verification Not Working

**Issue**: Verification email not received or link not working

**Solution**:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait up to 15 minutes for email delivery
- Verify you're checking the correct email account
- Request a new verification email
- Ensure the link hasn't expired (usually valid for 24 hours)
- Contact support@aceunlock.com for manual verification

### Profile Picture Won't Upload

**Issue**: Image upload fails or doesn't display

**Solution**:
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Verify file format (JPG, PNG, or GIF only)
- Try resizing the image to 400x400 pixels
- Use a different browser
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Compress the image using an online tool
- Contact support@aceunlock.com if problem continues

### Cannot Access Subscription Information

**Issue**: Subscription tab is empty or shows an error

**Solution**:
- Verify you have the correct permissions (Admin users only)
- Refresh the page
- Check if you're viewing the correct profile
- Recruiter users should contact their Admin for subscription details
- Contact support@aceunlock.com if you're an Admin and still can't access
