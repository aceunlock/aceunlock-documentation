## Best Practices for Success

### âœ… Do's
- **Start with Quick Create** for your first job (faster learning curve)
- **Watch candidate videos**, not just scores (personality and presence matter)
- **Review interviews within 24 hours** (candidates appreciate quick feedback)
- **Customize rubrics** to match your hiring priorities
- **Use specific scenario questions** (better than generic behavioral questions)
- **Set realistic score thresholds** (calibrate based on first 20-30 candidates)

### âŒ Don'ts
- **Don't skip the rubric** - It's critical for consistent evaluation
- **Don't use too many questions** - 5-8 questions is ideal (15-20 min interview)
- **Don't only rely on scores** - Watch videos to assess fit
- **Don't ignore schedule flexibility questions** (for roles requiring it)
- **Don't forget to invite your team** - Collaboration improves hiring decisions
