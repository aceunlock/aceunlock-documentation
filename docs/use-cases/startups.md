## Startup Success Tips

### 1. Define Your Criteria Early

**Before posting your first job**:
- List must-have vs. nice-to-have skills
- Create evaluation rubric (5-10 criteria max)
- Weight criteria by importance
- Share with co-founders for alignment

**Why it matters**: Consistent evaluation prevents "we'll know it when we see it" hiring.

### 2. Start Simple, Iterate

**Week 1**: Use default questions and rubrics
**Week 2**: Review first batch of interviews
**Week 3**: Refine questions based on results
**Month 2**: Optimize for your specific needs

**Pro tip**: Don't over-engineer on day one. Launch and learn.

### 3. Maintain Startup Culture

**Customize your**:
- Email templates (reflect your voice)
- Interview questions (reflect your values)
- Evaluation criteria (reflect your culture)

**Remember**: AI handles volume, but you define quality.

### 4. Move Fast on Top Talent

When you see a great candidate:
- Review transcript same day
- Reach out immediately
- Schedule next interview within 24 hours
- Make decisions quickly

**Reality**: The best candidates are off the market in 7-10 days.

### 5. Maintain Candidate Experience

Even at scale:
- Send rejection emails (they're automated!)
- Be transparent about timeline
- Provide feedback when possible
- Keep your careers page updated

**Why it matters**: Today's candidate could be tomorrow's customer or investor.
