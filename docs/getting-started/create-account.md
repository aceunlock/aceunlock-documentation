# Creating Your AceUnlock Account

Getting started with AceUnlock begins with creating your account. This process takes less than 2 minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A valid email address
- A strong password (at least 8 characters)
- Your organization name (if applicable)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Navigate to Sign Up

Visit [www.aceunlock.com/signup](https://www.aceunlock.com/signup) and click the "Get Started" button.

### 2. Enter Your Information

Fill in the registration form with:
- **Email address**: Use a work email for business accounts
- **Password**: Create a secure password
- **Full name**: Your first and last name
- **Company name**: Optional for individual users

### 3. Verify Your Email

After submitting the form:
1. Check your email inbox for a verification message
2. Click the verification link (valid for 24 hours)
3. You'll be redirected to your new dashboard

### 4. Complete Your Profile

On first login, you'll be prompted to:
- Set your timezone
- Choose your notification preferences
- Add a profile picture (optional)

## Troubleshooting

**Not receiving the verification email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Request a new verification email from the login page

**Password requirements not met?**
Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include at least one uppercase letter
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character
