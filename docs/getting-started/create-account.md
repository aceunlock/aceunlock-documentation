# Getting Started with AceUnlock

Welcome to AceUnlock! Getting started is easy and takes less than a few minutes. AceUnlock is a platform designed for companies and recruiters to make talent acquisition efficient by leveraging AI-powered interviewers and evaluators.

## Overview

AceUnlock streamlines your recruitment process through:
- **AI Interviewer**: Conducts automated, intelligent interviews with candidates
- **AI Evaluator**: Evaluates candidate responses based on customizable rubrics
- **Real-time Insights**: Monitor and manage interviews through comprehensive dashboards
- **Seamless Workflow**: From job posting to candidate selection, all in one platform

## Step 2: Activate Your Trial

1. Select your preferred plan on the [pricing page](https://www.aceunlock.com/pricing)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Try 7 days for free"**
3. Fill out the organization registration form with:
   - Company name
   - Your name and contact information
   - Work email address
   - Company size and industry (optional)
4. Click **"Activate Trial"** to submit your request

## Step 3: Check Your Email

After submitting the registration form:

- **Wait time**: Within minutes to a few hours
- **What to expect**: You'll receive an email invitation with a unique sign-up link for your company's AceUnlock site
- **Check spam folder**: If you don't see the email within a few hours, check your spam or junk folder

## Step 4: Complete Sign-Up

When you receive the invitation email:

1. **Click the invitation link** in the email
2. You'll be directed to a sign-up screen with two options:

### Option A: Sign Up with Google
- Click **"Sign up with Google"**
- Select your Google account
- Authorize AceUnlock access
- You'll be automatically signed in

### Option B: Sign Up with Email & Password
- Enter your **email address**
- Create a **secure password**
- Confirm your password
- Click **"Create Account"**

The entire sign-up process takes less than a minute to complete.

## Best Practices for Getting Started

### 1. Start with Sample Data
Review the pre-loaded evaluation rubrics and questions to understand how AceUnlock structures interviews and evaluations.

### 2. Test with Internal Candidates
Before going live, consider running a test interview with a team member to familiarize yourself with the candidate experience.

### 3. Customize Gradually
Start with the default settings and customize as you learn more about your specific needs.

### 4. Invite Your Team
Add team members early so they can collaborate on job postings and candidate evaluations.

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving the Invitation Email?

- **Check spam/junk folder**: Email filters may have caught the invitation
- **Verify email address**: Ensure you provided the correct email during registration
- **Wait time**: Allow up to 4 hours for processing during peak times
- **Contact support**: If you still haven't received the email, reach out to support@aceunlock.com

### Sign-Up Link Expired?

Invitation links are typically valid for 7 days. If yours has expired:
- Contact support@aceunlock.com to request a new invitation
- Include your company name and registration email

### Can't Access Google Sign-Up?

- Ensure you're using a Google Workspace or Gmail account
- Check your browser's popup blocker settings
- Try the email/password sign-up option instead
