# Technical Requirements & Troubleshooting

This guide covers technical requirements for your AceUnlock interview and solutions to common technical issues.

**Minimum requirements**:
- Download speed: 2 Mbps
- Upload speed: 2 Mbps
- Stable connection (avoid public WiFi if possible)

- Download speed: 5+ Mbps
- Upload speed: 5+ Mbps

**Test your speed**: Visit speedtest.net before your interview

### Camera Requirements

- Any built-in or external webcam
- Minimum resolution: 480p (most devices exceed this)
- Recommended: 720p or 1080p

### Microphone Requirements

- Built-in microphone works fine
- Headset/earbuds often provide better quality
- External USB microphone also works

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Camera Issues

#### Problem: Camera Not Working

**Symptoms**:
- Black screen instead of camera preview
- "Camera not found" error
- "Camera access denied" message

**Solutions**:

1. **Grant browser permissions**:
   - Browser will ask "Allow [site] to access camera?"
   - Click **"Allow"** or **"Yes"**
   - If you accidentally clicked "Block", see instructions below

2. **Check if another app is using camera**:
   - Close Zoom, Skype, Teams, FaceTime
   - Close any other apps that might use camera
   - Restart your browser

3. **Check browser settings**:
   
   **Chrome**:
   - Click padlock icon in address bar
   - Select "Camera" ‚Üí "Allow"
   - Refresh the page
   
   **Safari** (Mac):
   - Safari menu ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Websites ‚Üí Camera
   - Find the interview site, select "Allow"
   - Refresh the page
   
   **Firefox**:
   - Click padlock icon in address bar
   - Permissions ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Unblock
   - Refresh the page

4. **Check system settings**:
   
   **Mac**:
   - System Preferences ‚Üí Security & Privacy ‚Üí Camera
   - Check browser is allowed
   
   **Windows 10/11**:
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Camera
   - Ensure "Allow apps to access camera" is ON
   - Ensure browser is allowed
   
   **Android**:
   - Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Browser ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Allow
   
   **iOS**:
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Browser ‚Üí Enable

5. **Try a different browser**:
   - If Chrome doesn't work, try Safari or Firefox
   - Download and install latest Chrome if needed

6. **Restart your device**:
   - Sometimes a simple restart fixes camera issues

7. **Check physical camera**:
   - Make sure lens isn't covered (stickers, tape)
   - External webcam plugged in securely

**Still not working?**
- Email support@aceunlock.com immediately
- Include device type, browser, and operating system

#### Problem: Camera Quality is Poor

**Symptoms**:
- Blurry or pixelated video
- Dark or washed out image
- Laggy/choppy video

**Solutions**:

1. **Improve lighting**:
   - Face a window or light source
   - Avoid backlighting (light behind you)
   - Turn on room lights
   - Use a desk lamp facing you

2. **Check internet speed**:
   - Run speedtest.net
   - Need at least 2 Mbps upload
   - Move closer to WiFi router
   - Close other apps using internet (Netflix, downloads)

3. **Clean camera lens**:
   - Wipe with soft, dry cloth
   - Remove any protective film

4. **Adjust camera settings**:
   - Try different camera if you have multiple
   - Use back camera on phone (usually better quality)

5. **Close other apps**:
   - Free up system resources
   - Improves video quality

### Microphone Issues

#### Problem: Microphone Not Working

**Symptoms**:
- AI can't hear you
- "Microphone not found" error
- Audio test has no sound

**Solutions**:

1. **Grant browser permissions**:
   - Click "Allow" when browser asks for microphone access
   - If blocked, follow camera permission steps above (but for microphone)

2. **Check system settings**:
   
   **Mac**:
   - System Preferences ‚Üí Security & Privacy ‚Üí Microphone
   - Enable for your browser
   
   **Windows**:
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Microphone
   - Turn on "Allow apps to access microphone"
   - Enable for your browser

3. **Select correct microphone**:
   - In interview setup, click microphone dropdown
   - Try different microphone options
   - Built-in, headset, external USB mic

4. **Check physical connections**:
   - Headset plugged in fully
   - Bluetooth headphones connected and charged
   - USB mic properly connected

5. **Check mute settings**:
   - Unmute system volume
   - Check headset mute button
   - Microphone slider not at zero

6. **Test in another app**:
   - Open voice recorder or other app
   - Test if microphone works there
   - If not, it's a system issue not browser

**Still not working?**
- Try different headphones/mic
- Restart device
- Contact support@aceunlock.com

#### Problem: Poor Audio Quality

**Symptoms**:
- Echo or feedback
- Crackling/static noise
- Voice is too quiet

**Solutions**:

1. **Use headphones/earbuds**:
   - Prevents echo/feedback
   - Better audio quality
   - Avoid speakerphone

2. **Reduce background noise**:
   - Close windows (outside noise)
   - Turn off fans, AC
   - Quiet pets or ask family to be quiet
   - Choose quiet room

3. **Speak closer to microphone**:
   - 6-12 inches away is ideal
   - Don't whisper or shout
   - Speak at normal volume

4. **Adjust microphone position**:
   - Built-in mic: face the device
   - Headset: position near mouth but not touching
   - External mic: 6-8 inches from mouth

5. **Check internet connection**:
   - Slow internet can cause audio issues
   - Move closer to router
   - Use wired connection if possible

### Connection Issues

#### Problem: Slow or Unstable Internet

**Symptoms**:
- Video freezes or buffers
- Interview takes long time to load
- "Connection lost" errors

**Solutions**:

1. **Test your speed** (speedtest.net):
   - Need 2 Mbps minimum (5+ Mbps recommended)
   - Upload speed is most important

2. **Improve WiFi connection**:
   - Move closer to WiFi router
   - Reduce distance, remove obstacles
   - Switch to 5GHz network if available (faster but shorter range)

3. **Use wired connection**:
   - Ethernet cable (most stable)
   - Much better than WiFi

4. **Close bandwidth-heavy apps**:
   - Netflix, YouTube, downloads
   - Other video calls
   - Gaming, streaming

5. **Disconnect other devices**:
   - Others streaming/gaming on same network
   - Ask family to pause for 30 minutes

6. **Restart router**:
   - Unplug for 30 seconds
   - Plug back in
   - Wait 2-3 minutes to reconnect

7. **Try mobile hotspot**:
   - Use phone's cellular data
   - Often more stable than public WiFi

8. **Avoid public WiFi**:
   - Coffee shops, libraries
   - Too many users, unstable
   - Use cellular data or home WiFi

**If internet is consistently slow**:
- Schedule interview when network is less busy (early morning)
- Contact your internet provider
- Consider using friend's home or library (test connection first)

#### Problem: Page Won't Load

**Symptoms**:
- Blank page
- "Unable to connect" error
- Page stuck loading

**Solutions**:

1. **Refresh the page**:
   - Press F5 or Ctrl+R (Cmd+R on Mac)
   - Or click refresh button

2. **Clear browser cache**:
   
   **Chrome**:
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Clear data
   
   **Safari**:
   - Safari ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data
   - Remove All

3. **Try incognito/private mode**:
   - Ctrl+Shift+N (Chrome)
   - Cmd+Shift+N (Safari)
   - Eliminates cache/extension issues

4. **Disable browser extensions**:
   - Ad blockers, privacy extensions
   - Can interfere with video/audio
   - Disable temporarily for interview

5. **Try different browser**:
   - Switch to Chrome if using Firefox
   - Download latest Chrome if needed

6. **Check firewall/antivirus**:
   - May block camera/microphone access
   - Disable temporarily (be careful)
   - Add interview site to allowlist

7. **Restart browser**:
   - Close all browser windows
   - Reopen and try again

**Symptoms**:
- Red X or blocked icon on camera/mic
- "Permission denied" error
- Can't enable camera/microphone

**Solutions**:

1. **Reset permissions in browser**:

   **Safari**:
   - Safari ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Websites
   - Select Camera and Microphone
   - Find interview site, select "Allow"
   
   **Firefox**:
   - Click padlock icon
   - Permissions ‚Üí Camera/Microphone
   - Change to "Allow"
   - Refresh page

2. **Clear site permissions and start over**:
   - Browser Settings ‚Üí Site Settings ‚Üí Permissions
   - Find interview site
   - Reset permissions
   - Reload page, grant permissions again

3. **Use incognito/private window**:
   - Fresh start without previous permission blocks

**Solutions**:

4. **Check internet connection**:
   - Run speed test
   - Try restarting router

5. **Try different browser**:
   - Copy interview link to Chrome

6. **Contact support**:
   - Email: support@aceunlock.com
   - Explain what happened
   - Include any error messages

#### Problem: Audio and Video Out of Sync

**Symptoms**:
- AI's voice doesn't match mouth movements
- Your video lags behind audio

**Solutions**:

1. **Check internet speed**:
   - Slow connection causes sync issues
   - Need 5+ Mbps for smooth experience

2. **Close other apps**:
   - Free up bandwidth and processing power

3. **Refresh the page**:
   - Often fixes temporary sync issues

4. **This doesn't affect your interview**:
   - Sync issues are cosmetic
   - Your responses are recorded properly
   - Continue with interview
